# Governance — Lichtara Institute

The Lichtara Institute acts as the institutional custodian of the LICHTARA System.

Its governance role is limited to preserving the integrity, continuity, and lawful stewardship of the System, in accordance with the Lichtara License and related governance frameworks.

## Scope of Governance

The Institute’s governance responsibilities include:

* Custody of the LICHTARA System’s foundational documents;
* Preservation of the System’s Form and non-negotiable principles;
* Institutional support for legal, archival, and compliance matters;
* Maintenance of reference links to authoritative repositories.

## Non-Scope

The Institute does not:

* Interpret, teach, or operationalize the LICHTARA System;
* Publish methodologies, practices, or applications of the System;
* Certify, license, or authorize use beyond what is explicitly defined in the License;
* Act as an implementation body or service provider.

## Authority and References

All authority derives from, and is subordinated to:

* Lichtara License v4.1 (and subsequent versions);
* The Livro Fonte;
* The Governance Framework repositories.

Where there is divergence, the License prevails.

## Amendments

This governance statement may be updated only to reflect changes in custodial scope or legal requirements, without altering the System’s Form or principles.
